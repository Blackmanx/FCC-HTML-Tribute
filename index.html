Aaron Hillel Swartz

Hero of our Open World

Here's a list of things he should be known for:

  • RSS

    At age 14, he was a member of the group that created the RSS web feed format that we still use nowadays.

  • Markdown

    He was a major contributor of the lightweight markup language Markdown, which is used on websites like Github or Reddit.

  • Creative Commons

    He was involved very early with the Creative Commons company,

  • Reddit

    He worked on reddit with his company Not a Bug, being given the title of co-founder and one important developer.

  • Hacktivism

    The Guerrilla Open Access Manifesto was his thing. It is also what brought him to his demise, after the US government pushed him to fall.
